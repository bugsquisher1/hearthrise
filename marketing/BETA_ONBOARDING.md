# Hearthrise Beta — Welcome

Thanks for signing up to test Hearthrise before launch. This doc tells you what to expect, how to give feedback that actually helps, and the few rules we ask you to follow.

## What is Hearthrise?

A single-player + lightly-online cozy idle RPG. You train skills, fight monsters, build a homestead, and — importantly — make progress even when you're not playing. Think of it as something between an idle clicker and a classic MMO skill grind.

## What we're testing

This soft beta is a **pre-1.0 build**. The core loops (combat, gathering, cooking, crafting, dungeons, market, multi-character) are in. We're looking for:

1. **Crashes and broken features** — anything that doesn't work, doesn't render, or doesn't save.
2. **Balance feedback** — does any skill feel too slow? Too fast? Are dungeons fair? Are food buffs strong enough?
3. **First-hour experience** — was the tutorial useful? Did anything confuse you in the first 30 minutes?
4. **What's missing** — content gaps, dead-end items, wishlist features.

We are **not** looking for design rewrites. The shape of the game is locked. If you'd like to suggest a feature for v2, write it down and we'll add it to a list.

## What's not in yet

Heads up — the following are intentionally missing from beta and are not bugs:

* **Cloud save** is opt-in and may lag the local save by 30s.
* **Mobile builds** may be a separate build from the Steam beta.
* **Localization** — English only at launch.
* **Sound effects** — placeholder/silent in some menus. Music is final.
* **Anti-cheat** — disabled in beta. Please don't deliberately exploit, but if you find one, report it.
* **Endgame past level 99** is not in scope for 1.0.
* **Raids (4-player)** are post-launch.

## How to report bugs

Inside the game: **Settings → Send Feedback**. Or if it's already crashed, fill out this form: [LINK]

A bug report we love includes:

1. What you were doing when it broke.
2. What you expected to happen.
3. What actually happened.
4. Screenshot or 10-second clip if possible.
5. Your character slot and approximate playtime.

If the game crashed, screenshots of the developer console (F12 in browser builds) are gold.

## What you get

Beta participation is voluntary, but as thanks we are committing to:

1. **A free copy of the launch build** for everyone who submits at least one bug report.
2. **An exclusive beta-tester cosmetic** (a unique trophy + theme) on launch accounts that linked to the beta sign-up.
3. **Recognition** — opt in if you want your in-game character name in the credits.

## Beta rules

1. **No NDA.** Stream, video, and post about Hearthrise freely. Just please tag content as "beta build" so non-testers know what they're seeing isn't final.
2. **Don't share your beta access key** — if you want a friend in, send them the public sign-up link.
3. **Don't deliberately exploit** the economy or share exploit guides publicly. If you find an exploit, report it before posting about it.
4. **Be kind to other testers.** The Discord and any in-game chat are subject to the published Code of Conduct. Trolling, harassment, slurs, and threats get an instant ban with no appeal.
5. **Refunds:** the beta itself has no money exchanged. If you buy any in-game item with real money during the beta, that purchase will be honored at launch — but we strongly recommend waiting until 1.0 ships.

## Build cadence

We aim to ship a new build to beta every 7–10 days. Each build comes with patch notes posted to Discord and a copy in your in-game **Settings → What's New** panel. Saves carry forward through migrations; if your save ever stops loading, restore the most recent backup via **Settings → Save Backups**.

## Final notes

Two weeks of soft beta. Then a feedback gap, a polish pass, and a launch. We're excited you're here. Find every bug, tell us every paper cut, and have fun.

— The Hearthrise team
