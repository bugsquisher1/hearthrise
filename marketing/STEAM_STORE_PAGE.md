[h1]Hearthrise — a cozy idle homestead RPG[/h1]

Run a small homestead at the edge of a fading world. Train Woodcutting, Mining, Cooking, Smithing, Crafting, and a dozen other skills. Hunt slimes, goblins, dragons, and the things that come after dragons. Cook food that buffs your party, smith weapons that turn the tide, and build a house that quietly buffs every skill you'll ever train.

The game keeps progressing while you sleep — when you log back in, you find a summary of what your character did with the time, what they earned, and what they need from you next.

[h2]Features[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]14 skills[/b] across combat, gathering, processing, and homestead trades. Each skill levels 1–99 with deep-cut content at every milestone.
[*][b]Three combat styles[/b] — sword, ranged, magic — each with its own progression curve and gear path.
[*][b]30+ monsters[/b] across six tiers, with weakness types, family-locked loot, and dungeon-only variants.
[*][b]6 instanced dungeons[/b] gated by Bind-on-Pickup keys that drop from matching monsters. Run them solo for guaranteed loot, or scavenger-style for skill-driven gear quality.
[*][b]Recipe-driven cooking, smithing, and crafting[/b] — every food gives a real, stacking gameplay buff. Every weapon you forge has a tier-curved stat line. Recipes for endgame gear drop from boss mobs you'll need to actually beat.
[*][b]Homestead system[/b] — 8 rooms × 3 tiers, each granting an account-wide bonus. Decorate with themes, plant a working farm in the yard, and place trophies from your kills.
[*][b]Bounty Hunter career[/b] — its own skill tree, its own progression, its own warrants and rewards.
[*][b]Player market[/b] — listing-based exchange with a flat tax and Bind-on-Pickup protection. No direct trading means no scams.
[*][b]Up to 5 characters per account[/b] with shared cosmetics and entitlements but per-character gold, gear, and skills.
[*][b]True offline progression[/b] — combat, gathering, and crafting all continue at the same rate as active play. Up to 12 hours base, 16 with Offline+.
[/list]

[h2]How it plays[/h2]

You pick a skill, you watch the bar fill, and you collect what falls out. Or you pick a fight, watch the damage numbers, and pick the next fight. The game grades up steadily, never rushes you, and rewards you for being patient.

If you want it to play itself for an afternoon, it will. If you want to sit down and grind a 4-hour boss farm session, it can do that too. Hearthrise is designed for both moods.

[h2]No ads. No energy timers. No FOMO.[/h2]

We sell cosmetics, character slots, and a one-time premium pack. That's it. No roulette boxes, no battle passes that expire, no daily-login chains that punish you for missing a day. The game is the game.
