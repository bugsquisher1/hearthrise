## 2. What information we collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. The categories below describe everything we may receive when you play Hearthrise.

**a. Account information.** If you create an online Hearthrise account (optional), we receive the email address or sign-in identifier you provide via Google, Apple, or Steam OAuth, plus a randomly-generated player ID. We do not store passwords; authentication is handled by the third party you sign in with.

**b. Game state.** Save data — including your character's level, inventory, equipped gear, completed quests, and gameplay statistics — is stored locally on your device. If you opt into cloud save, an encrypted snapshot is stored on our servers (operated by Supabase) so you can sync between devices.

**c. Telemetry.** We collect non-identifying gameplay events (which tab you open, how long sessions last, which monsters you fight, whether you complete the tutorial) to help us tune balance and identify bugs. Telemetry is tied to a randomly-generated session ID, not to you personally.

**d. Crash reports.** If the game crashes, we receive a report containing the error message, a stack trace, the game version, your operating system version, and game state at the time of the crash (your active skill, monster, gold, kills, character slot). Crash reports are processed by Sentry and retained for 90 days.

**e. Purchases.** If you buy something through Steam, the Apple App Store, the Google Play Store, or another platform, the platform handles payment processing and shares with us only the receipt confirmation and entitlement (e.g., "this player owns the Hearth Hall Premium pack"). We do not receive credit card numbers or billing addresses.

**f. Support correspondence.** If you contact us by email, we keep your message and our reply for as long as needed to resolve the issue and for up to 24 months afterward.

We do not collect, store, or process your real name, physical address, phone number, government ID, biometric data, or precise location. We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

## 3. How we use the information

We use the information described above to:

* Run the game, save your progress, and sync between devices.
* Verify your purchases and grant entitlements (e.g., Hearth Hall Premium).
* Diagnose crashes and bugs.
* Tune game balance based on aggregate telemetry.
* Respond to your support requests.
* Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information to build advertising profiles, and Hearthrise products are ad-free.

## 4. Legal bases (EU/UK)

If you are in the EU, UK, or another jurisdiction with similar laws, we rely on the following legal bases under Article 6 of the GDPR:

* **Performance of a contract** — for processing necessary to run the game and your account.
* **Legitimate interests** — for telemetry, crash reporting, and security, balanced against your privacy rights.
* **Consent** — for any processing you've explicitly opted into (e.g., cloud save).
* **Legal obligation** — for tax records, fraud prevention, and complying with court orders.

You can withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing. See **Your rights** below.

## 5. Sharing with third parties

We share information only with the service providers needed to operate the game:

* **Supabase** — cloud save storage and authentication.
* **Sentry** — crash and error reporting.
* **Steam, Apple App Store, Google Play** — payment processing for purchases.

These providers act as data processors under our instructions and are bound by their own privacy policies and data-processing agreements. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies beyond those listed above.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users or the public.
