## Key features

**1. True idle progression.** Combat, gathering, and crafting all advance at the same rate whether you're playing actively or away. 12-hour offline cap base, 16 with Offline+. The welcome-back screen tells you exactly what your character did with the time.

**2. Fourteen skills.** Combat (attack / strength / defense / hitpoints / ranged / magic / prayer), gathering (woodcutting / mining / fishing / farming), processing (cooking / smithing / crafting), plus the bounty-hunter career skill. All level 1–99 with deep cuts at every milestone.

**3. Three combat styles.** Sword, ranged, magic — each with its own training curve, gear path, and weapons-style weakness rules per monster.

**4. Real cooking buffs.** Every food you cook grants a stacking gameplay buff with a real duration timer. Tomato Soup gives +10% monster respawn. Dragon Stew gives +10% combat XP. Hunter's Feast gives +15% monster respawn for 15 minutes.

**5. Six instanced dungeons.** Bind-on-Pickup keys drop from matching monster families. Run dungeons two ways — automated for guaranteed loot, or scavenger-style with a six-minute time budget where your skill levels determine the gear quality of what you bring to the boss.

**6. Up to five characters per account.** Shared cosmetics + entitlements, but per-character gold, gear, skills. Five-slot hard cap is intentional — character identity matters in a long-running idle game.

**7. Listing-based player market.** No direct trading, no scams. 1.5% flat tax, 48-hour listing TTL, twelve listing slots per character. Bind-on-Pickup items can never be flipped.

**8. Homestead system.** Eight rooms × three tiers, each granting an account-wide bonus. The Garden buffs farming yield. The Forge buffs smithing speed. The Trophy Room buffs combat XP. Decorate with theme packs, plant a working farm in the yard.

**9. No ads, no FOMO.** No daily login chains that punish missing a day. No expiring battle passes. No loot boxes. We sell cosmetics, character slots, and one premium pack. That's it.

## What inspired the game

Hearthrise grew out of a frustration with two genres of game. Idle/incremental games are honest about being clickers but rarely have any depth past hour 50. Classic MMO grind games have endless depth but demand more time than most adults can give. Hearthrise sits in the middle: it is honest about being an idle game, but the skill design is borrowed from games where leveling 1 to 99 means something.

Major reference points: RuneScape's skill grind, Stardew Valley's homestead pacing, Idle Clans' party-based combat, Melvor Idle's offline progression, A Dark Room's text-driven discovery rhythm.

## FAQ

**Is this game pay-to-win?**
No. Premium currency (gems) buys cosmetics, character slots, and convenience items (extra inventory tabs). It cannot be exchanged for gold, gear, or progression. The Hearth Hall Premium pack unlocks some character slots and a permanent +33% offline cap; that's the closest thing to a power purchase, and it's a one-time pack.

**Will there be loot boxes?**
No.

**Is there a battle pass?**
No.

**Does the game work offline?**
Yes — completely offline single-player. Online features (cloud save, leaderboards, market) require an account, but you can play indefinitely without one.

**How long is it?**
Active playtime to level 99 in all skills is targeted at ~10 months at moderate engagement (~1 hr active + 12 hrs offline per day). Casual players can hit max combat in 4–6 weeks.

**Does it support controllers?**
Limited at launch. Full controller support is planned for a post-launch update.

**Can I play with friends?**
Asynchronously, yes — through the listing-based market and clan leaderboards. Real-time co-op (4-player raids) is a post-launch feature.

**Why no real-time PvP?**
Idle games are not built for real-time PvP, and we'd rather do nothing than do it badly.

**Will my save carry over from beta to 1.0?**
Yes. Save migrations are versioned and tested. Worst case, you can restore from an automatic backup via Settings.

**Localization?**
English at launch. Major Steam languages (FR, DE, ES, BR-PT, JA, zh-Hans) within six months of launch.
